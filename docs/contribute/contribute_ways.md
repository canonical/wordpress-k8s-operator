## Ways to contribute

* {ref}`Contribute to development <how_to_contribute_dev>`
* {ref}`Contribute to documentation <how_to_contribute>`

Generally, before developing enhancements to this charm, you should consider
[opening an issue](https://github.com/canonical/wordpress-k8s-operator/issues)
explaining your use case.

If you would like to chat with us about your use-cases or proposed implementation,
you can reach us on the
[Canonical Matrix public channel](https://matrix.to/#/#charmhub-charmdev:ubuntu.com)
or [Discourse](https://discourse.charmhub.io/).
